About Me

My name is Arsly Mwamwaya, a Bachelor of Education in Geography student at the University of Malawi. I was born on 21 January 2003 and was born and raised in Mzuzu. I have also spent some years in Chitipa and different parts of Southern Malawi, experiences that have helped me understand diverse communities and environments.

I am passionate about education, geography, and continuous personal growth. My goal is to develop my professionalism as an educator and contribute towards creating meaningful learning experiences for students in Malawi and wherever my teaching journey may take me.

As a student, I am open to volunteering opportunities in teaching and educational activities where I can gain experience, share knowledge, and contribute positively to learners and communities.

I believe that every student deserves quality education and the opportunity to discover their potential. Through teaching, I hope to inspire learners to experience the value and greatness of education.
